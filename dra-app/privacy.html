Privacy Statement

We are committed to protecting your personal information. Where we collect personal information about you as part of your survey responses, we will treat the information in accordance with the Australian Privacy Principles established by the Commonwealth Privacy Act 1988.

Where KPMG collects personal information as part of this survey, we do so for the purposes of authenticating your access to the platform. Individuals and individual responses will not be identifiable in the reporting provided back to your organisation.
